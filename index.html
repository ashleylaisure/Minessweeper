How To Play Minesweeper:

Minesweeper is a logic game with hidden mines scattered across the board. It's your job to avoid them.

  • Click on a square to reveal what's underneath.
  • If the square contains a mine, the game ends and you lose.
  • If the square doesn't contain a mine, it will reveal a number or nothing.
  • If the square has a number, this number represents how many mines are in the neighboring 8 squares.
  • Start by clicking randomly.
  • Use the numbers as clues as to how many mines are in the surrounding 8 squares.
  • Right Click to place a flag where you think a mine is located.
  • The mines counter will decrease with each potional mine avoided.
  • You win when all safe squares are uncoverd and all mines have been flagged.
